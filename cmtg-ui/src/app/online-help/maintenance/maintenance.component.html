Functional Description

The Maintenance panel provides unit status information for the currently selected Gateway Controller.
Refer to the following for information on this panel : Maintenance Panel for GWC on GENIUS.

The Menu

Maintenance Panel for GWC on GENIUS

The Maintenance panel for the C20a Platform provides unit status information and maintenance functions for the currently selected Gateway Controller. The name and unit IP addresses are displayed for the currently selected gateway controller at the top of the main panel. A status bar, located at the bottom of the main panel, displays operation messages. The previous twenty messages may be displayed via the dropdown list.

Status Fields

The status fields are updated on a regular basis by the system, or may be updated via selection of the Refresh menu item located under the Windows menu.

Overall Readliness State

  • In Service:
    • Both GWC units are In Service.
  • Degraded:
    • One GWC unit is In Service, the other Out of Service.
  • Out of Service:
    • Both GWC units are In Service.
  • Unknown:
    • Both GWC units are In Service.

    Readiness State

  • In Service:
    • The GWC unit is In Service.
  • Out of Service:
    • The GWC unit is Out of Service.

    High Availability State

  • Active:
    • The GWC unit is in charge of the call processing and maintenance operations for the pair. The mate unit should be "standby".
  • Inactive:
    • The GWC unit is acting as backup to the operation of the mate unit, which should be "active". Also referred to as "inactive".

    Administrative State

  • Unlocked:
    • The GWC unit is administratively permitted to perform services for its users. This is independent of its inherent operability.
  • Locked:
    • The GWC unit is administratively prohibited from performing services for its users.

    Operational State

  • Enabled:
    • The GWC unit is partially or fully providing service.
  • Disabled:
    • The GWC unit is not providing service.

    Alarm State

  • critical(1), major(2), minor(3), alarmOutstanding(4)
    • The severity of currently raised alarms.

    Maintenance Actions

    For the C20a Platform all maintenance actions are performed on the NDM.
