Perform the following steps to complete this procedure.

At the TMM client window

1. Set Auto Refresh Value

  1. Click the "Preferences" link on the right top side of the window:

  2. Choose a new value from the drop-down menu and press "Save".

2. Turn Auto Refresh On or Off

  1. Click the "Preferences" link on the right top side of the window:

  2. Click on the "Auto-Refresh" check box and press "Save"

3. Need Confirmation
When Need Confirmation toggled on, confirmation dialog will be displayed for every operation.
